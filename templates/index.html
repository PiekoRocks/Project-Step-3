                                    About StreamLine

Our StreamLine application is designed to manage data for over 5,000 fire hydrants across urban, 
suburban, and rural areas. Fire hydrants require regular maintenance, including inspections and repairs 
performed by field workers. To ensure operational efficiency and make it easier to keep hydrants working 
for emergencies, the status of these hydrants must be tracked, allowing field workers to prioritize 
their tasks effectively. 

StreamLine will serve as a centralized hub for hydrant data, consolidating key details such as unique 
IDs, locations, and hydrant types. The problem with current databases is that most information about 
hydrants is stored in multiple systems (spreadsheets, handwritten logs, legacy software). This makes it 
challenging to access reliable and comprehensive hydrant information when needed.

The solution is to create a centralized fire hydrant database powered by StreamLine. This platform will 
automate manual tasks such as scheduling maintenance and inspections, reducing human error and 
increasing efficiency. Hydrants can be tracked in real-time, with a map-based interface providing 
up-to-date locations and operational statuses.
